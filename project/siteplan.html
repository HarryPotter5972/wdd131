Site Name:

The site name is: Zelda Lore Compendium

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to explore the lore of The Legend of Zelda franchise by Nintendo following the course of the official Zelda Timeline in Hyrule Historia with additions including games released after the book's publication. Lore within the games themselves including worldbuilding, in-game map analyses, and explanations for in-game navigation distortions within the world of the games.

Scenarios:

  • What is the identity of the Hero's Shade from Twilight Princess?

Color Scheme:

The main color scheme would be green, red, and gold. The background would be green with section titles being red and main text being gold.

Typography:

The text font used would be Times New Roman.
